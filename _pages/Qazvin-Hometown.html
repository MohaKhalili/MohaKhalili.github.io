Qazvin, my hometown, is a special city. Qazvin is the largest city and capital of the province of Qazvin in Iran.

Qazvin is a traditional city and has almost religious people. The people give a lot of importance to the family and love it. Qazvin hasn't many tall building but instead, the city has beautiful architectural texture like Caravanserai of Sa'd al-Saltaneh.

The climate is cold but dry, due to its position south of the Alborz mountains. Because of its climate, Qazvin has beautiful mountainous nature called Alamut. Alamut region is a great place in northern Qazvin and there are many historical castles and lakes in it, which attracts many tourists from all over the world.
